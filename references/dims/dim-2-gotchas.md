# Dim 2 — Gotchas Section

The highest-signal content in any skill. Should reflect real Claude failures.

**Score 5 — Excellent**
- Has a dedicated `## Gotchas` section with 3+ items
- Items are specific and actionable (not vague warnings)
- Clearly derived from real failures, not hypotheticals
- Grows over time (evidence of iteration)

**Score 4 — Good**
- Has a Gotchas section with 2+ items, but some are still somewhat generic
- Clearly intended to capture real failures, even if not all are specific yet

**Score 3 — Acceptable**
- Has some gotchas scattered inline, but not in a dedicated section
- Items are somewhat vague ("be careful with X")

**Score 2 — Weak**
- One or two inline warnings, clearly hypothetical rather than from experience

**Score 1 — Poor**
- No gotchas section at all
- Generic warnings not specific to this domain

**Key questions:**
- [ ] Is there a dedicated Gotchas section?
- [ ] Are items specific enough to prevent real Claude mistakes?
- [ ] Does it feel like it was built from experience, not guesswork?
